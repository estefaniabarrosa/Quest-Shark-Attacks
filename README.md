# 🦈 Shark Attack Analysis & Surf School Strategy (Brazil)

This project explores shark attack data to evaluate whether opening a surf school in Brazil is a viable and safe business opportunity.

Instead of focusing only on risk, the analysis aims to understand **where, when, and how that risk can be managed** through data-driven decisions.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

Surfing naturally involves risk…  
but that risk is not evenly distributed.

This project analyzes shark attack patterns in Brazil to:
- Identify high-risk locations
- Understand seasonal trends
- Evaluate whether risk is increasing or stable over time
- Translate insights into business decisions

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Shark attack risk in Brazil is **not evenly distributed**
- Certain areas (e.g., Pernambuco) show significantly higher risk
- Risk varies throughout the year, with identifiable peak periods
- No strong upward trend → risk is **relatively stable over time**

👉 This means risk is **not random — it is manageable**

---

## 💼 Business Conclusion

Opening a surf school in Brazil is:

✔️ **Viable**  
✔️ **Strategic (if location & timing are chosen carefully)**  
✔️ **An opportunity backed by data**

Key recommendations:
- Avoid high-risk areas
- Operate during lower-risk months
- Use data to inform safety protocols and positioning
